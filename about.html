About Nandini Dairy

Nandini Dairy is a symbol of trust and purity across millions of households in Karnataka. With a legacy built on the dedication of farmers and the commitment to quality, we offer a range of dairy products that are fresh, hygienic, and rich in nutrition. Our milk and dairy items are sourced directly from cooperative farmers and processed with utmost care. Nandini is the flagship brand of Karnataka Milk Federation (KMF), established in 1974 under the "Operation Flood" program initiated by Dr. Verghese Kurien. The goal was to empower rural milk producers and ensure a steady supply of quality dairy to urban areas.Today, KMF stands as the second-largest milk cooperative in India, only after Amul. Its journey has helped millions of rural families earn a stable income through dairy farming.
Daily Milk Collection System Milk is collected twice a day ‚Äì in the early morning and evening ‚Äì from over 25,000 milk producers' cooperative societies in rural Karnataka. Over 80 to 90 lakh liters of milk are procured daily. Each village has a milk collection center equipped with: Electronic Milk Analyzers (for testing fat/SNF) Weighing machines Bulk Milk Coolers (BMCs) Chilling units
